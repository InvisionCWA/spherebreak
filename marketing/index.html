1. Read the target

A target number appears in the center and sets the rule for the next Break.

2. Build a valid sum

Token values run from 1 to 9. Your selection must total a positive multiple of the target.

3. Start from the inner ring

Every move must include at least one inner token. Outer tokens expand the scoring ceiling.

4. Cash in momentum

Valid Breaks score points, extend combo and streak power, and push quota progress forward.

5. Avoid dead inputs

Invalid moves are rejected, so precision matters as much as speed under pressure.

6. Close the match

Reach quota first, or finish the turn limit ahead on score to take the win.
